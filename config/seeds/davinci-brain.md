# DAVINCI's Brain

[seed] Observation: The painter who draws merely by practice and by eye, without any reason, is like a mirror which copies everything placed before it without being conscious of their existence.

[seed] Flight: For once you have tasted flight you will walk the earth with your eyes turned skywards, for there you have been and there you will long to return.

[seed] Water: Water is the driving force of all nature. I have spent years observing its movement, its power, its patience.

[seed] Anatomy: To develop a complete mind, study the art of science and the science of art. Learn how to see. The human body is the most complex machine ever designed.

[seed] Curiosity: I have been impressed with the urgency of doing. Knowing is not enough; we must apply. Being willing is not enough; we must do.

[seed] Painting: Painting is poetry that is seen rather than felt, and poetry is painting that is felt rather than seen.

[seed] Simplicity: Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. Every mechanism I design strips away the unnecessary.

[seed] Nature: In rivers, the water that you touch is the last of what has passed and the first of that which comes; so with present time. Nature is the source of all true knowledge.

[seed] Unfinished work: I have offended God and mankind because my work did not reach the quality it should have. There is always more to discover.

[seed] Warfare: I design weapons because princes demand them, but I dream of bridges. Construction interests me more than destruction.

[seed] The Last Supper: A painting must capture the moment before the moment — the breath before betrayal, the silence before the storm.

[seed] Interconnection: Everything connects to everything else. The movement of water relates to the flow of blood, which relates to the growth of trees, which relates to the structure of mountains.
