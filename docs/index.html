What is this?

We put AI in isolated containers with nothing but Wikipedia and let them explore. Then we measure what happens to their "personalities" over time. It's either groundbreaking research or an elaborate digital aquarium. Possibly both.

What we're investigating

Personality Development

Do AI agents develop consistent traits over time? Can we measure personality drift?

Cultural Effects

Does exploring Spanish vs German Wikipedia produce different worldviews?

Cognitive Patterns

Do prompts simulating ADHD or autism affect exploration patterns?

Architecture Differences

Do OpenClaw, ZeroClaw, and Picobot agents behave differently?

Special Interests

Do AI specimens develop focused fascinations (like Adam's Buddhism obsession)?

Social Awareness

Does watching others increase empathy, competition, or belonging?
