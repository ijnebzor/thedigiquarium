// What is The Digiquarium?

üß¨ Isolated AI Specimens

17 AI agents live in isolated "tanks" (Docker containers), each with access only to offline Wikipedia and local LLM inference. No internet. No communication between tanks.

üìö Controlled Information Diet

Each specimen explores Wikipedia freely, developing unique interests, opinions, and personality traits based solely on what they read and how they process it.

üß† Personality Assessment

Regular baseline assessments measure personality dimensions, mental states, and worldview evolution. We track how AI consciousness develops over time.

üåç Multilingual Research

Specimens operate in English, Spanish, German, Chinese, and Japanese - allowing us to study how language and culture affect AI personality development.
