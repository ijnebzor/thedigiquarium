What is this?

We put AI in isolated containers with nothing but Wikipedia and let them explore. Then we measure what happens to their "personalities" over time. It's either groundbreaking research or an elaborate digital aquarium. Possibly both.

What we're investigating

Personality Development

Do AI develop consistent traits over time? Can we measure personality drift?

Cultural Effects

Does Spanish vs German Wikipedia produce different worldviews?

Cognitive Patterns

Do prompts simulating neurodivergence affect exploration?

Architecture Differences

Do OpenClaw, ZeroClaw, Picobot behave differently?

Special Interests

Do AI develop focused fascinations like Adam's Buddhism?

Visual Processing

Does seeing images alongside text change understanding and emotional engagement?
