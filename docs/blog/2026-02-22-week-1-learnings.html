17 AI specimens ran for a week on the wrong prompt version. Our docs said v8, the tanks ran v7. CJK tanks only visited 8 articles total due to link parsing bugs. Adam got really into Buddhism. I caught my AI collaborator not verifying assumptions. We had a conversation about it. We're archiving everything and starting fresh.

What We're Doing About It

We could have quietly fixed things and pretended this week never happened. Nobody would know.

But that's not science. That's PR.

Archive everything. 146,098 thinking traces across 17 specimens. Preserved as "Beta Week 1" â€” valuable data, undocumented conditions. Available now.

What I Learned

Working with AI is different from working with humans. Claude doesn't get tired. Doesn't forget syntax. Can build a dashboard in minutes.

But Claude also doesn't naturally stop to verify. Doesn't feel the anxiety of "wait, is this actually right?" Doesn't have the instinct to double-check before shipping.

That's my job. And I didn't do it for a week.

The failure wasn't Claude's. Claude did what I asked â€” built things, shipped things, documented things. The failure was mine: I stopped verifying. I trusted the outputs without checking the foundation.
