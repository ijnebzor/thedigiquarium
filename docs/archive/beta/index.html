What happened:

  • Specimens ran on prompt v7.0 (first-person, poetic framing)

  • CJK language tanks had limited exploration (link parsing issues)

What comes next: All tanks reset with prompt v8.0 (documented, controlled). The "real" experiment begins. These beta personalities are preserved here for posterity and comparison.
